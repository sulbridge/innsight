FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what partners ask most before working with us.

How fast can you unlock a blocked ad account or BM?
+
Most account and Business Manager unlocks are resolved within 24–72 hours depending on the platform and type of restriction. Complex cases involving policy escalations may take up to 5–7 business days. We provide status updates at every step.
Is there a minimum ad spend to work with INNSIGHT?
+
For managed media buying partnerships, our minimum is $10K/month in ad spend. For account solutions (rentals, BM sales, unlocks, credit lines), there is no minimum — we work with buyers and agencies of all sizes.
What verticals do you support for account rentals?
+
We provide verified, warmed-up accounts for virtually any vertical — e-commerce, lead gen, finance, health & wellness, gaming, apps, and more. Each account comes with clean history and is matched to your specific niche requirements.
How do credit lines and ad spend discounts work?
+
We offer extended payment terms (Net 7 to Net 30) on ad platform spend, plus volume-based discounts that lower your effective media cost. Terms depend on your monthly spend level and payment history. Contact us for a personalized quote.
Do you offer white-label services for agencies?
+
Yes. We operate fully under your brand — your clients will never know we exist. We handle media buying, account management, reporting, and optimization while you maintain the client relationship. Full NDA and confidentiality guaranteed.
What guarantees do you provide?
+
For account unlocks, we operate on a success-based model — you only pay if the account is restored. For managed campaigns, we commit to transparent KPIs and reporting from day one. No long-term lock-in contracts — results keep partners, not paperwork.
